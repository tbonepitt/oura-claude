Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 21, 2025  ·  Operated by SaintlyDigital.com

🔒 The short version: Your Oura token is stored only in your own browser. Your health data is never stored on our servers — it flows from the Oura API directly to your screen and nowhere else.

1. Who We Are

Oura Health Dashboard ("the App", "we", "us") is a web application operated by SaintlyDigital.com that visualizes your Oura Ring health data. We are not affiliated with Oura Health Oy.

2. What Data We Access

When you connect your Oura Ring, the App accesses the following data from the Oura API on your behalf:

  • Daily sleep scores and sleep stage data (deep, REM, light, awake)
  • Daily readiness scores and contributing factors
  • Daily activity scores, step counts, and calorie data
  • Heart rate and heart rate variability (HRV) data
  • Blood oxygen saturation (SpO2) data
  • Stress and recovery metrics
  • Cardiovascular age estimates
  • VO2 Max estimates
  • Personal information (first name) for dashboard personalization

This data is accessed in real time when you load the dashboard and is used solely to generate the visualizations and insights you see on screen.

3. How Your Token Is Stored

Your Oura Personal Access Token is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. It is:

  • Never transmitted to our servers — it is sent only from your browser directly to the Oura API
  • Never logged or recorded by any component of this application
  • Fully under your control — you can remove it at any time by clicking "⚙ Token" in the dashboard header

4. How We Process Your Data

When you load the dashboard, your browser sends your token as a request header to our serverless API function hosted on Vercel. The function uses that token to query the Oura API, performs statistical computations (correlations, forecasts, anomaly detection), and returns the results to your browser. No health data is written to any database, log, or storage at any point. Each request is stateless and ephemeral.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your personal or health data to any third party, including advertisers or data brokers. The only external services your data touches are:

  • Oura API (api.ouraring.com) — to retrieve your health metrics
  • Vercel — our serverless hosting provider, which processes requests in memory only and retains no data

6. Oura's Collection of Usage Data (Required Disclosure)

As required by the Oura API Agreement, we are required to disclose the following: Oura may collect certain usage data and information related to our use of the Oura API Materials and Oura Platform in connection with this application. Oura may use such data for any business purpose, including providing enhancements to the Oura API and Platform. This data is collected by Oura independently and is governed by Oura's Privacy Policy.

8. Analytics and Tracking

This App does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics services, or any form of user tracking. We do not know who uses the App or how often.

9. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has used this App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

10. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since we store no personal data on our servers, there is nothing for us to delete or provide on request. To remove all data associated with your use of this App:

  • Click "⚙ Token" in the dashboard header and remove your token — this immediately stops all data access
  • Clear your browser's localStorage for this site to remove the locally stored token
  • Revoke the Personal Access Token at cloud.ouraring.com/personal-access-tokens
  • Manage your Oura account directly at cloud.ouraring.com

Upon request, we will confirm in writing that we hold no data about you. For rights related to data held by Oura Health Oy, please refer to Oura's Privacy Policy.

11. Oura Branding Compliance

This App uses the Oura API in accordance with the Oura API Agreement and Oura Brand Guidelines. We are not affiliated with or endorsed by Oura Health Oy. "Oura" is a trademark of Oura Health Oy.

12. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. We notify Oura of any security breach involving Oura data within 24 hours of discovery, as required by the Oura API Agreement and GDPR Article 32(1). Our serverless functions run in isolated execution environments and do not store credentials or health data anywhere.
