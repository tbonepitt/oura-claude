What's New

Release history for Ring Edge
Everything built this weekend — March 2026
  • Insights as landing page — Recovery Intelligence, correlations, and sleep patterns front and center — not raw daily scores.
  • Recovery Intelligence — Personal sleep target, cumulative debt, payback plan, recovery rate, trajectory badges, and your personal records (readiness, HRV, deep sleep).
  • Weekly Sleep Patterns — Bar chart of avg sleep score by day of week, best/worst day highlighted.
  • Sleep-stage HR chart — Heart rate during sleep colored by sleep stage (deep/REM/light/awake).
  • 7-Day Readiness Forecast — Predicted from your 60-day personal patterns.
  • Sleep Science tab — Deep sleep decoder with personalized findings and best/worst night analysis.
  • Anomaly detection — Flags unusual drops with auto-detected likely causes.
  • Vitals tab — SpO2, VO2 max, stress/recovery balance, cardio age.
  • Resilience score — Sleep recovery, daytime recovery, and stress contributions.
  • Profile tab — Personal info from your Oura account, ring details, and personal records.
  • Smart bedtime — "Get In Bed By" accounts for your avg time to fall asleep.
  • Data freshness bar — Shows exactly when sleep vs activity data was last synced.
  • Honest data positioning — Oura's API is daily, not real-time. Removed all "live" language.
  • Name personalization — Header uses your name derived from your Oura account.
  • Sleep debt +/− trend — Last night's debt vs the night before with direction indicator.
  • Browser token saving — Setup prompts you to save your token so you don't re-enter it.
  • Feedback widget — 👍/👎 rating + optional comment so we can keep improving.
